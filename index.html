ABOUT ME

Hello, I am a Computing Science student at Simon Fraser University. As a developer with an interest in design and human computer interactions, I have taken a variety of courses in graphic design, visual communications, and user interface design to broaden my knowledge.

Over the years, I have experience working in PostgreSQL, command line, bash scripts, on virtual machines, and various types of probes. My past projects have been written in Python, C, C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Ruby on Rails, Unity, and Processing.

While I am a fan of Sherlock and the Harry Potter series, I also enjoy a variety of different activities such as hiking, swimming, or ice-skating.
