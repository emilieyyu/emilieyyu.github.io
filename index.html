about myself

Hello, I am a Computing Science student at Simon Fraser University in Vancouver, BC. My projects range from web development, interactive shooting games, Natural Language Processing textual analysis to designing mobile applications, and poster pamphlets.

As a Software Test Engineer at Sierra Wireless, I helped run regression and stress tests as well as automate testcases in Python and C# for embedded modules. At CloudPBX, I conducted research to install various monitoring and statistical collection tools to provide better VoIP services via CLI on DigitalOcean Ubuntu. I created bash scripts to automate the process of importing and exporting metrics to the database, and displayed results on graphs and maps. From these past experiences, I have worked with PostgreSQL, Command Line Interface, Visual Studio, various Virtual Machines, RaspberryPi, and developed Python, C#, and Bash scripts.

While I am a fan of Sherlock and the Harry Potter series, I enjoy photography and indulge in various sports including hiking, swimming, ice-skating, and snowboarding.
